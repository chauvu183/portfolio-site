THE CHALLENGE

A message fails — but where, across five systems?

EDI business messages — purchase orders, shipment notices, status updates — travel through 5+ internal systems before they're done: IB (EDI gateway), KNMS (enterprise service bus), JMS (async messaging), MFT (file transfer) and XLOG (trip tracking).

When one stalled, operations teams had to chase logs across disparate tools to work out where and why it broke. Diagnosis was slow and error-prone, and a single stuck message could take hours to resolve.

SOLUTION OVERVIEW

One timeline for a message's entire journey

Jupiter turns opaque, multi-system failures into a single readable flow. Each node is a system, each step a hop — with status, payload and file details a click away. From one screen, ops teams detect a failure, diagnose its root cause, and reprocess the stuck message.
